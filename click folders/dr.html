One Piece

The story follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young boy whose body gains rubber-like properties after consuming a Devil Fruit. Luffy sets out on a grand adventure to become the King of Pirates by finding the legendary treasure known as the "One Piece," left behind by the Pirate King, Gol D. Roger. To achieve his goal, Luffy assembles a diverse crew, known as the Straw Hat Pirates, each with unique skills and dreams. Together, they explore the dangerous Grand Line, encountering other pirates, powerful enemies, and the World Government.
